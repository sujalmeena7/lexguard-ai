Security Overview

LexGuard AI is built with an uncompromising focus on the security of your legal assets. We employ multiple layers of technical safeguards to ensure that your documents remain private and protected.

Ephemeral Data shredding

Documents uploaded for audit are stored in a session-scoped vector index. Once the audit is complete or the browser window is closed, the index is programmatically shredded. We maintain a zero-retention policy for raw document content.

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted between your browser and our hybrid backend (Render/Streamlit) is protected by industry-standard TLS 1.3 encryption. We ensure that your data is never sent over unencrypted channels.

Rate Limiting & Anti-Abuse

Our API layer (FastAPI) utilizes SlowAPI for granular rate limiting. This protects the platform against brute-force attempts and ensures fair resource allocation for all enterprise users.

No Model Persistence

We leverage Llama 3 70B via Groq LPU™ technology. Data is processed in-memory for inference and is not saved to disk by the inference engine, ensuring that your legal secrets never leak into model weights.

Enterprise Compliance

For organizations requiring SOC2 Type II or ISO 27001 mapping, please contact our enterprise security team for a full technical audit pack.
